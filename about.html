< About Me />

A Sofware Craftperson, a Stuntman and a Loverboy OG.

I love code. I love to take raw ideas and build great products with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I know JavaScript is imperfect, but I embrace its functional side and genuinely enjoy coding with it. I like to talk about obscure computer science topics, but really, I just want to write simple code and ship new products and features to customers (or improve existing ones). I love great UX. I spend most of your time coding, but I also have an eye for great design and a feel for great UX. I aspire to become a designer but my creative outlet has always been the code. Either way, I care about more than just the code. I love learning new things. I love playing with new tech. I focus is on frontend JavaScript code, but I can dig in wherever needed. Python, CSS, build tools, you name it. If I don't already know it, I'm ready to learn it. I can balance lots of concerns. Frontend apps have to take into account performance (using networks and devices that we can't control), customer demands, A/B tests, UX research, code quality, a rapidly changing ecosystem of languages and modules, and the list goes on. I can balance those demands without getting overwhelmed and keep the needle moving forward. I love helping others. I love sharing knowledge with the rest of my team, giving back to the open-source community, and directly helping customers.
